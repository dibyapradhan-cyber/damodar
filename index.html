Published Works by Damodar Pradhan

About the Author

Damodar Pradhan is listed on Academia.edu as an Independent Researcher with research interests in Buddhism, Buddhology, and Newar Buddhism. He has 235 followers on the platform. He is based in Alberta, Canada, and identifies himself as a Monumental Guide in his published writings — someone deeply connected to Kathmandu's Buddhist heritage sites, particularly the Thambahi/Bhagwan Bahal complex in Thamel. 5. नेवा: भाय् म्हिग:, थौं व कन्हुय्! (Nepal Bhasa — Yesterday, Today and Tomorrow)

  • Published in Kisali Magazine, pages 80–83
  • Written in Nepal Bhasa (Newari script with Devanagari)
  • Topics covered: The history of Nepal Bhasa suppression under Shah and Rana rulers, UNESCO's classification of Nepal Bhasa as endangered, global preservation efforts including ISO recognition (ISO 639-2/3 "new"), Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia (72,822+ articles as of 2024), online dictionaries, Google Translate inclusion (2024), Nepal Bhasa Academy's cultural encyclopedia, and AI (Artificial Intelligence) in Nepal Bhasa.
